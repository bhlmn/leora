Home

My purpose is to enhance how you experience the spaces you spend your life in.

By decluttering, organizing, rearranging, and even decorating, your space can be transformed into a functional and inspiring environment. Together we can create systems that work well for you and hold up over time, allowing you to focus on the things that matter most.

Services

How can I brighten your world?

I take great care in trying to understand your needs—determining which solutions might be most useful, and creating a plan based on your available resources. The information below is intended to give you an idea of what I can do for you. If you need a service that isn’t listed, please ask! If I am unable to provide a service I will gladly put you in touch with someone who can.

Organizing & decluttering

This is a great option for those that feel as though the list of to-dos will never end. If you have found you don’t have enough storage, it may be that you have more things than you need. Purging before the holidays can be a great way to prepare for all the exciting new things that are coming your way.

Redesign

Rearranging can breathe new life into a settled space. Let’s switch up the flow of a room to create the prefect location for your next event. In the market for a new piece? With some creative maneuvers you might find that you already have everything you need at your fingertips.

Decorating

Our surroundings have a significant impact on our outlook and productivity. The design of your environment can be inspiring and energizing. I would love to join your hunt for pieces that truly reflect your personality and elevate the space.

Putting it together

I am happy to support you in the implementation and installation of the systems and designs we create together. I would love to give you a hand with your DIY project, furniture assembly and more.

Preparing to move

From decluttering, downsizing, and preparing to stage your home for sale, I would love to help make the transition into your new space as light as possible. Space planning and layout can be optimized by conducting a walk-through of the new location together before the movers arrive.

Hosting & event planning

Having a party or hosting an event? Let’s walk through the venue and make sure we make the most of your space and create an intentional flow for guests and activities. Design and organization will refine the atmosphere and keep the celebration in focus.
