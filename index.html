Current Collaborations

The desire to connect, create, and serve has provided Jenni with incredible opportunities to support local organizations in unexpected ways!

Leora Organization & Redesign

As the owner and principal consultant at Leora Organization & Redesign, Jenni works alongside managers, small business owners, and individuals: assessing dysfunctional workspaces, redesigning for improved workflow, and providing training and support for team members as they adjust.

Beaverton Downtown Association

A fanatic supporter of small businesses, Jenni has volunteered with Beaverton’s Main Street America Affiliate since 2019. Originally the chair of the Design Committee, she served as assistant project manager for the Cosign Program. Now, as Training Director, she oversees the on-boarding, training, and support of new volunteers.

Women to Women Network

Jenni is the program coordinator for Coffee & Conversation, the largest monthly gathering of Tualatin’s Women to Women Network. As a member of the leadership team, she helps brainstorm and execute the organization’s long-term strategies for elevating women in business.

CMK Connects

Serving as a business ambassador, a facilitator of Power Partner Circles and a regular contributor to Net & Learns, Jenni is a valuable resource to budding businesses in the Hillsboro area looking to make the most of their limited assets.

WFG National Title Oregon

Real Estate agents can receive CE credits by attending Jenni’s course, Leveraging Communication and Organization in your Listings. Owner occupied listings can provide unique challenges for staging. Jenni’s class and listing evaluations are tools available to agents looking to strengthen client relationships while helping sellers with their exit strategy.

About Jenni

An active and engaged learner, Jenni regularly expands her knowledge at regional and national conferences, online classes, and Toastmasters. She holds a Masters in Human Resources Management from the Florida Institute of Technology, a Bachelors in Psychology from Portland State University, and a Project Management Principles and Practices Specialization from University of California, Irvine.

In a perfect world everyone has the opportunity to feel valued and appreciated for the skills they are willing to share, environments are designed to activate the potential of the people who use them, and people find meaning and purpose in the work they show up to do. This vision moves beyond functional design and requires attention to productivity, motivation, satisfaction, communication, and leadership.

It is the pursuit of this ideal that led Jenni to create Leora—to provide opportunities to do meaningful and impactful work. But it is the work, not the business, that fuels her drive. The time has come to join a team and contribute to the momentum of something bigger; to become an asset in helping your business thrive.
